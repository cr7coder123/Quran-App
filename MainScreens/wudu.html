HOW TO DO WUDU
NAMES QIBLA WUDU PRAY
NASHEEDS TASBIH ZAKAT HAJJ
EXTRA
You only need to do each step once but doing it 2-3 times is recommended because you get more reward. STEP 1 Wash both your hands up to your wrist three times. STEP 2 Rinse the mouth three times and clean it thoroughly. STEP 3 Snuff water in the nostrills three times. STEP 4 Wash the face three times from the hair of the forehead to under the chin and from one ear to the other. STEP 5 Wash both arms from the fingertips to the elbow 3 times. First the right arm then the left. STEP 6 Wipe the head by doing this: wet your palms, set apart the thumbs and forefinger, place the other fingers on the edge of the head and move to the back of the head STEP 7 Then clean the inside of the ears with your forefinger, and rub the back of ears with the thumb. STEP 8 Then wash the feet with the left hand upto above the ankles. Wash your right foot then the left foot.
