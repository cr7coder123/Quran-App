5 PILLARS OF ISLAM
FIRST PILLAR TESTIMONY/SHAHADA لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا الله مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ الله There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad(SAW) is the Messenger of Allah.
SECOND PILLAR PRAYER/SALAH Praying 5 times a day; Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.
THIRD PILLAR FASTING Fasting is the practice of not eating or drinking. During the holy month of Ramadan, Muslims fast for 30 days and each day they fast from before sunrise, to sunset.
FOURTH PILLAR ZAKAAT Zakat requires all Muslims to donate a portion of their wealth to charity. Muslims must meet a certain threshold before they can qualify for zakat. The amount is 2.5% of an individual's total savings and wealth.
FIFTH PILLAR HAJJ Hajj is a mandatory religious duty for Muslims that must be carried out at least once in their lifetime by all adult Muslims who are physically and financially capable of undertaking the journey, and of supporting their family during their absence from home. Hajj is a pilgrimage made to the Kaaba, the "House of Allah," in the sacred city of Mecca in Saudi Arabia.
