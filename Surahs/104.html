Surah Al Humazah
QURAN

Chapter 104, Verse 1 وَيْلٌۭ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٍۢ لُّمَزَةٍ Woe to every scorner and mocker
Chapter 104, Verse 2 ٱلَّذِى جَمَعَ مَالًۭا وَعَدَّدَهُۥ Who collects wealth and [continuously] counts it.
Chapter 104, Verse 3 يَحْسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُۥٓ أَخْلَدَهُۥ He thinks that his wealth will make him immortal.
Chapter 104, Verse 4 كَلَّا ۖ لَيُنبَذَنَّ فِى ٱلْحُطَمَةِ No! He will surely be thrown into the Crusher.
Chapter 104, Verse 5 وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْحُطَمَةُ And what can make you know what is the Crusher?
Chapter 104, Verse 6 نَارُ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمُوقَدَةُ It is the fire of Allah, [eternally] fueled,
Chapter 104, Verse 7 ٱلَّتِى تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى ٱلْأَفْـِٔدَةِ Which mounts directed at the hearts.
Chapter 104, Verse 8 إِنَّهَا عَلَيْهِم مُّؤْصَدَةٌۭ Indeed, it [i.e., Hellfire] will be closed down upon them
Chapter 104, Verse 9 فِى عَمَدٍۢ مُّمَدَّدَةٍۭ In extended columns.
