Chapter 105, Verse 1 أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَابِ ٱلْفِيلِ Have you (O Muhammad) not seen how your Lord dealt with the Owners of the Elephant?

Chapter 105, Verse 5 فَجَعَلَهُمْ كَعَصْفٍۢ مَّأْكُولٍۢ And made them like eaten straw.
