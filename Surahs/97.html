Chapter 97, Verse 1 إِنَّا أَنزَلْنَاهُ فِى لَيْلَةِ ٱلْقَدْرِ Indeed, We sent it [the Qur'an] down during the Night of Decree.
Chapter 97, Verse 2 وَمَآ أَدْرَاكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ And what can make you know what is the Night of Decree?
Chapter 97, Verse 3 لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ خَيْرٌۭ مِّنْ أَلْفِ شَهْرٍ The Night of Decree is better than a thousand months.
Chapter 97, Verse 4 تَنَزَّلُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ فِيهَا بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِم مِّنْ كُلِّ أَمْرٍ The angels and the Spirit descend therein by permission of their Lord for every matter.
Chapter 97, Verse 5 سَلاَمٌۭ هِىَ حَتَّىٰ مَطْلَعِ ٱلْفَجْرِ Peace it is until the emergence of dawn.
