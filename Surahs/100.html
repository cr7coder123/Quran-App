Chapter 100, Verse 1 وَٱلْعَـٰدِيَـٰتِ ضَبْحًا By the racers, panting,
Chapter 100, Verse 2 فَٱلْمُورِيَـٰتِ قَدْحًا And the producers of sparks [when] striking,
Chapter 100, Verse 3 فَٱلْمُغِيرَٰتِ صُبْحًا And the chargers at dawn,
Chapter 100, Verse 4 فَأَثَرْنَ بِهِۦ نَقْعًا Stirring up thereby [clouds of] dust,
Chapter 100, Verse 5 فَوَسَطْنَ بِهِۦ جَمْعًا Arriving thereby in the center collectively,
Chapter 100, Verse 6 إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ لِرَبِّهِۦ لَكَنُودٌ Indeed mankind, to his Lord, is ungrateful.
Chapter 100, Verse 7 وَإِنَّهُۥ عَلَىٰ ذَٰلِكَ لَشَهِيدٌ And indeed, he is to that a witness.
Chapter 100, Verse 8 وَإِنَّهُۥ لِحُبِّ ٱلْخَيْرِ لَشَدِيدٌ And indeed, he is, in love of wealth, intense.
Chapter 100, Verse 9 أَفَلَا يَعْلَمُ إِذَا بُعْثِرَ مَا فِى ٱلْقُبُورِ But does he not know that when the contents of the graves are scattered
Chapter 100, Verse 10 وَحُصِّلَ مَا فِى ٱلصُّدُورِ And that within the breasts is obtained,
Chapter 100, Verse 11 إِنَّ رَبَّهُم بِهِمْ يَوْمَئِذٍۢ لَّخَبِيرٌۭ Indeed, their Lord with them, that Day, is [fully] Acquainted.
