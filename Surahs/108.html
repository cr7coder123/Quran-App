Chapter 108, Verse 1 إِنَّآ أَعْطَيْنَٰكَ ٱلْكَوْثَرَ Indeed, We have granted you, [O Muhammad], al-Kawthar.
Chapter 108, Verse 2 فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ So pray to your Lord and sacrifice [to Him alone].
Chapter 108, Verse 3 إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلْأَبْتَرُ Indeed, your enemy is the one cut off.
