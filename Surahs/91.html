Chapter 91, Verse 1 وَٱلشَّمْسِ وَضُحَىٰهَا By the sun and its brightness,
Chapter 91, Verse 2 وَٱلْقَمَرِ إِذَا تَلَىٰهَا And [by] the moon when it follows it,
Chapter 91, Verse 3 وَٱلنَّهَارِ إِذَا جَلَّىٰهَا And [by] the day when it displays it,
Chapter 91, Verse 4 وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰهَا And [by] the night when it covers it,
Chapter 91, Verse 5 وَٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا بَنَىٰهَا And [by] the sky and He who constructed it,
Chapter 91, Verse 6 وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا طَحَىٰهَا And [by] the earth and He who spread it,
Chapter 91, Verse 7 وَنَفْسٍۢ وَمَا سَوَّىٰهَا And [by] the soul and He who proportioned it,
Chapter 91, Verse 8 فَأَلْهَمَهَا فُجُورَهَا وَتَقْوَىٰهَا And inspired it [with discernment of] its wickedness and its righteousness,
Chapter 91, Verse 9 قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّىٰهَا He has succeeded who purifies it,
Chapter 91, Verse 10 وَقَدْ خَابَ مَن دَسَّىٰهَا And he has failed who instills it [with corruption].
Chapter 91, Verse 11 كَذَّبَتْ ثَمُودُ بِطَغْوَىٰهَا Thamud denied [their prophet] by reason of their transgression,
Chapter 91, Verse 12 إِذِ ٱنۢبَعَثَ أَشْقَىٰهَا When the most wretched of them was sent forth,
Chapter 91, Verse 13 فَقَالَ لَهُمْ رَسُولُ ٱللَّهِ نَاقَةَ ٱللَّهِ وَسُقْيَٰهَا And the messenger of Allah [Salih] said to them, "[Do not harm] the she-camel of Allah or [prevent her from] her drink."
Chapter 91, Verse 14 فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَعَقَرُوهَا فَدَمْدَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ رَبُّهُم بِذَنبِهِمْ فَسَوَّىٰهَا But they denied him and hamstrung her. So their Lord brought down upon them destruction for their sin and made it equal [upon all of them].
Chapter 91, Verse 15 وَلَا يَخَافُ عُقْبَٰهَا And He does not fear the consequence thereof.
