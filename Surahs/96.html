Chapter 96, Verse 1 ٱقْرَأْ بِٱسْمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ Recite in the name of your Lord who created.
Chapter 96, Verse 2 خَلَقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مِنْ عَلَقٍ Created man from a clinging clot.
Chapter 96, Verse 3 ٱقْرَأْ وَرَبُّكَ ٱلْأَكْرَمُ Recite, and your Lord is the most Generous
Chapter 96, Verse 4 ٱلَّذِى عَلَّمَ بِٱلْقَلَمِ Who taught by the pen –
Chapter 96, Verse 5 عَلَّمَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مَا لَمْ يَعْلَمْ Taught man that which he knew not.
Chapter 96, Verse 6 كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ لَيَطْغَىٰٓ No! [But] indeed, man transgresses
Chapter 96, Verse 7 أَنْ رَءَاهُ ٱلْسَّتَغْنَىٰٓ Because he sees himself self-sufficient.
Chapter 96, Verse 8 إِنَّ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ رُجْعَىٰٓ Indeed, to your Lord is the return.
Chapter 96, Verse 9 أَرَءَيْتَ ٱلَّذِى يَنْهَىٰ Have you seen the one who forbids
Chapter 96, Verse 10 عَبْدًۭا إِذَا صَلَّىٰ A servant when he prays?
Chapter 96, Verse 11 أَرَءَيْتَ إِن كَانَ عَلَى ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ What if this ˹servant˺ is ˹rightly˺ guided,
Chapter 96, Verse 12 أَوْ أَمَرَ بِٱلتَّقْوَىٰٓ or encourages righteousness?
Chapter 96, Verse 13 أَرَءَيْتَ إِن كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ Have you seen if he denies and turns away?
Chapter 96, Verse 14 أَلَمْ يَعْلَم بِأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَرَىٰ Does he not know that Allah sees?
Chapter 96, Verse 15 كَلَّآ لَئِن لَّمْ يَنتَهِ لَنَسْفَعًۭا بِالنَّاصِيَةِ No! If he does not desist, We will surely drag him by the forelock –
Chapter 96, Verse 16 نَاصِيَةٍۢ كَـٰذِبَةٍۢ خَاطِئَةٍۢ A lying, sinning forelock.
Chapter 96, Verse 17 فَلْيَدْعُ نَادِيَهُۥ Then let him call his associates –
Chapter 96, Verse 18 سَنَدْعُ ٱلزَّبَانِيَةَ We will call the wardens of Hell.
Chapter 96, Verse 19 كَلَّا لَا تُطِعْهُ وَٱسْجُدْ وَٱقْتَرِب Again, no! Never obey him ˹O Prophet˺! Rather, ˹continue to˺ prostrate and draw near ˹to Allah˺.
