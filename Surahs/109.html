Chapter 109, Verse 1 قُلْ يَآ أَيُّهَا ٱلْكَٰفِرُونَ Say, "O disbelievers,
Chapter 109, Verse 2 لَآ أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ I do not worship what you worship.
Chapter 109, Verse 3 وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ Nor are you worshipers of what I worship.
Chapter 109, Verse 4 وَلَآ أَنَاۤ عَٰبِدٌۭ مَّا عَبَدتُّمْ And I will not be a worshiper of what you worship.
Chapter 109, Verse 5 وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ Nor will you be worshipers of what I worship.
Chapter 109, Verse 6 لَكُمْ دِينُكُمْ وَلِىَ دِينِ For you is your religion, and for me is my religion."
