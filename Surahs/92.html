Chapter 92, Verse 1 وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰ By the night when it covers,
Chapter 92, Verse 2 وَٱلنَّهَارِ إِذَا تَجَلَّىٰ And [by] the day when it appears,
Chapter 92, Verse 3 وَمَا خَلَقَ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلْأُنثَىٰٓ And [by] He who created the male and female,
Chapter 92, Verse 4 إِنَّ سَعْيَكُمْ لَشَتَّىٰ Indeed, your efforts are diverse.
Chapter 92, Verse 5 فَأَمَّا مَنْ أَعْطَىٰ وَٱتَّقَىٰ As for he who gives and fears Allah
Chapter 92, Verse 6 وَصَدَّقَ بِٱلْحُسْنَىٰ And believes in the best [reward],
Chapter 92, Verse 7 فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُۥ لِلْيُسْرَىٰ We will ease him toward ease.
Chapter 92, Verse 8 وَأَمَّا مَنۢ بَخِلَ وَٱسْتَغْنَىٰ But as for he who withholds and considers himself free of need,
Chapter 92, Verse 9 وَكَذَّبَ بِٱلْحُسْنَىٰ And denies the best [reward],
Chapter 92, Verse 10 فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُۥ لِلْعُسْرَىٰ We will ease him toward difficulty.
Chapter 92, Verse 11 وَمَا يُغْنِى عَنْهُ مَالُهُۥٓ إِذَا تَرَدَّىٰٓ And what will his wealth avail him when he falls?
Chapter 92, Verse 12 إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا لَلْهُدَىٰ Indeed, [incumbent] upon Us is guidance.
Chapter 92, Verse 13 وَإِنَّ لَنَا لَلْءَاخِرَةَ وَٱلْأُولَىٰ And indeed, to Us belongs the Hereafter and the first [life].
Chapter 92, Verse 14 فَأَنذَرْتُكُمْ نَارًۭا تَلَظَّىٰ So I have warned you of a Fire which is blazing.
Chapter 92, Verse 15 لَا يَصْلَىٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلْأَشْقَى None will [enter to] burn therein except the most wretched one
Chapter 92, Verse 16 ٱلَّذِى كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ Who had denied and turned away.
Chapter 92, Verse 17 وَسَيُجَنَّبُهَا ٱلْأَتْقَى But the righteous one will be spared it -
Chapter 92, Verse 18 ٱلَّذِى يُؤْتِى مَالَهُۥ يَتَزَكَّىٰ [He] who gives his wealth to purify himself
Chapter 92, Verse 19 وَمَا لِأَحَدٍ عِندَهُۥ مِن نِّعْمَةٍۢ تُجْزَىٰٓ And not [giving] for anyone who has [done him] a favor to be rewarded
Chapter 92, Verse 20 إِلَّا ٱبْتِغَآءَ وَجْهِ رَبِّهِ ٱلْأَعْلَىٰ But only seeking the countenance of his Lord, Most High.
Chapter 92, Verse 21 وَلَسَوْفَ يَرْضَىٰ And he is going to be satisfied.
