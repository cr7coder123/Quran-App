Chapter 102, Verse 1 أَلْهَىٰكُمُ ٱلتَّكَاثُرُ Competition in [worldly] increase diverts you
Chapter 102, Verse 2 حَتَّىٰ زُرْتُمُ ٱلْمَقَابِرَ Until you visit the graveyards.
Chapter 102, Verse 3 كَلَّا سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ No! You are going to know.
Chapter 102, Verse 4 ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ Then no! You are going to know.
Chapter 102, Verse 5 كَلَّا لَوْ تَعْلَمُونَ عِلْمَ ٱلْيَقِينِ No! If you only knew with knowledge of certainty...
Chapter 102, Verse 6 لَتَرَوُنَّ ٱلْجَحِيمَ You will surely see the Hellfire.
Chapter 102, Verse 7 ثُمَّ لَتَرَوُنَّهَا عَيْنَ ٱلْيَقِينِ Then you will surely see it with the eye of certainty.
Chapter 102, Verse 8 ثُمَّ لَتُسْـَٔلُنَّ يَوْمَئِذٍ عَنِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ Then you will surely be asked that Day about pleasure.
