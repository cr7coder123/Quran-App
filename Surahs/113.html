Chapter 113, Verse 1 قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ Say, "I seek refuge in the Lord of the Daybreak,"
Chapter 113, Verse 2 مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ From the evil of what He has created,
Chapter 113, Verse 3 وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ And from the evil of the darkness when it settles,
Chapter 113, Verse 4 وَمِن شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِى الْعُقَدِ And from the evil of the blowers in knots,
Chapter 113, Verse 5 وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ And from the evil of the envious when they envy."
