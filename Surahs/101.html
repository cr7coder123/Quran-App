Chapter 101, Verse 1 ٱلْقَارِعَةُ The Striking Calamity -
Chapter 101, Verse 2 مَا ٱلْقَارِعَةُ What is the Striking Calamity?
Chapter 101, Verse 3 وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا ٱلْقَارِعَةُ And what can make you know what is the Striking Calamity?
Chapter 101, Verse 4 يَوْمَ يَكُونُ ٱلنَّاسُ كَٱلْفَرَاشِ ٱلْمَبْثُوثِ It is the Day when people will be like moths, dispersed,
Chapter 101, Verse 5 وَتَكُونُ ٱلْجِبَالُ كَٱلْعِهْنِ ٱلْمَنفُوشِ And the mountains will be like wool, fluffed up.
Chapter 101, Verse 6 فَأَمَّا مَن ثَقُلَتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ Then as for one whose scales are heavy [with good deeds],
Chapter 101, Verse 7 فَهُوَ فِى عِيشَةٍۢ رَّاضِيَةٍۢ He will be in a pleasant life.
Chapter 101, Verse 8 وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَفَّتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ But as for one whose scales are light,
Chapter 101, Verse 9 فَأُمُّهُۥ هَاوِيَةٌۭ His refuge will be an abyss.
Chapter 101, Verse 10 وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا هِيَهْ And what can make you know what that is?
Chapter 101, Verse 11 نَارٌ حَامِيَةٌۭ It is a Fire, intensely hot.
