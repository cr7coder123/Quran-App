Chapter 93, Verse 1 وَٱلضُّحَىٰ By the morning brightness
Chapter 93, Verse 2 وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا سَجَىٰ And [by] the night when it covers with darkness,
Chapter 93, Verse 3 مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَىٰ Your Lord has not taken leave of you, [O Muhammad], nor has He detested [you].
Chapter 93, Verse 4 وَلَلْـَٔاخِرَةُ خَيْرٌۭ لَّكَ مِنَ ٱلْأُولَىٰ And the Hereafter is better for you than the first [life].
Chapter 93, Verse 5 وَلَسَوْفَ يُعْطِيكَ رَبُّكَ فَتَرْضَىٰٓ And your Lord is going to give you, and you will be satisfied.
Chapter 93, Verse 6 أَلَمْ يَجِدْكَ يَتِيمًۭا فَـَٔاوَىٰ Did He not find you an orphan and give [you] refuge?
Chapter 93, Verse 7 وَوَجَدَكَ ضَآلًّۭا فَهَدَىٰ And He found you lost and guided [you],
Chapter 93, Verse 8 وَوَجَدَكَ عَآئِلًۭا فَأَغْنَىٰ And He found you poor and made [you] self-sufficient.
Chapter 93, Verse 9 فَأَمَّا ٱلْيَتِيمَ فَلَا تَقْهَرْ So as for the orphan, do not oppress [him].
Chapter 93, Verse 10 وَأَمَّا ٱلسَّآئِلَ فَلَا تَنْهَرْ And as for the petitioner, do not repel [him].
Chapter 93, Verse 11 وَأَمَّا بِنِعْمَةِ رَبِّكَ فَحَدِّثْ But as for the favor of your Lord, report [it].
